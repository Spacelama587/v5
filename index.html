AI Leaderboard

CleverFeed's MeritChat AI ranks the quality of your participation in discussions.

Write Compelling Statements

The AI engine reads your comments and evaluates how compelling, thoughtful or otherwise impressive they are. Each contribution is analyzed for depth, originality, and impact on the discussion.

Receive Responses

When someone responds to your comments, the AI engine evaluates that response to further learn the value of your comment. If you cause someone's opinion to evolve, MeritChat AI takes note and adjusts your influence score accordingly.

Engage in Discussions

The more engaging the conversation, the greater the chance your comments influence others. Top influencers give thoughtful and meaningful responses, creating a vibrant community of ideas and perspectives.

Community Guidelines

Our engine detects unkind behavior. Bad language and hate speech are not allowed. Users who act undesirably may be banned, and their history removed to maintain a respectful environment.
